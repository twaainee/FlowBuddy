The Methodology

The Science of Flow

Camera Integration

Requesting user permission to access the mobile device's camera hardware and stream the real-time visual feed into a customized application window, providing a live viewfinder for immediate framing, exposure adjustment, or continuous visual monitoring.

Pose Detection

Real-time, on-device machine learning solution that detects human body keypoints (landmarks) and projects a visual skeletal structure onto the video stream.

Basic Form Checks

To improve the accuracy of your postural assessment, apply at least two to three distinct checks for every pose by calculating the specific angles of key joints. By quantifying these movements such as the degree of knee flexion or the tilt of the spine you can move beyond visual guesswork and ensure the alignment meets exact biomechanical standards.

Visual Feedback

Implement a visual feedback system using color-coded indicators Green, Yellow, and Red to provide users with immediate, intuitive assessments of their performance. This traffic-light approach translates complex angle data into simple cues.

Text Tips

Integrate real-time, on-screen correction messages that provide users with clear, actionable instructions to fix their form instantly. Instead of just identifying an error, these prompts should act as a virtual coach, guiding the user toward a "Green" state with simple cues like "Lift your chest" or "Lower your hips."
