Privacy Policy

Your camera stays with you.

FlowBuddy is designed around local, browser-based pose detection. Your video is used for live alignment feedback and is not recorded, uploaded, or stored by FlowBuddy.

No Recording

FlowBuddy does not record camera video, take snapshots, or save biometric media.

On Device

Pose landmarks are calculated in your browser during the live session.

Local Summaries

Practice scores are saved only in this browser so you can view recent progress.

Camera Access

FlowBuddy asks for camera permission only when you start a live practice. The camera feed is used to show your viewfinder and run pose detection locally in your browser. You can deny permission, close the page, or end the session at any time.

What FlowBuddy Does Not Collect

FlowBuddy does not collect names, accounts, email addresses, camera recordings, photos, audio, or biometric profiles. No camera feed is sent to a FlowBuddy server.

Session Summaries

When a practice ends, FlowBuddy saves a small summary in your browser storage: date, duration, alignment score, pose name, and correction focus. This progress history stays on your device, is not uploaded, and can be cleared from the Progress page.

Third-Party Libraries

FlowBuddy uses MediaPipe pose detection scripts loaded from a CDN. The pose analysis still runs inside your browser. If the model cannot load, the camera screen shows an error instead of starting the session.

Health And Safety

FlowBuddy provides educational alignment feedback and is not medical advice. Stop if you feel pain, dizziness, or discomfort, and speak with a qualified professional before practicing with injuries or medical conditions.

Your Controls

You can block camera permission in your browser, use the End Practice control to turn the camera off, clear local progress from the Progress page, or close the browser tab. If camera access is blocked, FlowBuddy provides steps to update permission safely.
