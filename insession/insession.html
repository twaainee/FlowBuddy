Privacy & Terms of Use

Please read before starting your practice

Your camera feed is processed entirely on your device. No video, image, or biometric data is ever recorded, transmitted, or stored outside this browser session.

1. Camera Access

FlowBuddy requests access to your device camera solely to detect your body pose in real time. The video stream is processed locally by Google's MediaPipe Pose library, which runs entirely within your browser. At no point is any video data sent to FlowBuddy servers, third-party services, or any external destination.

2. Data Collection

FlowBuddy does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. No account is required. No cookies are set. Your session results (duration and alignment score) are passed between pages using URL parameters only, and are discarded when you close the browser tab.

3. On-Device Processing

All pose analysis — including landmark detection, angle calculation, and form feedback — is computed in real time on your own device using WebAssembly and JavaScript. This means your video never leaves your machine, even for analysis.

4. Health Disclaimer

FlowBuddy is an educational tool and is not a substitute for professional medical or fitness advice. If you have any injuries, medical conditions, or physical limitations, please consult a qualified healthcare provider before participating in any yoga or exercise activity.

5. Your Consent

By checking the box below and clicking "Allow Camera Access", you confirm that you have read and understood these terms, and that you consent to FlowBuddy using your device camera for local, real-time pose detection only.
